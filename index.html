Summer

This summer even though Covid existed was honestly probably one of the best summers of my life. Me and my group of friends got super close and became almost like brothers. I worked out everyday with them, we hung out almost everyday, and I even went on vacation with a couple of them. Brandon Rice, James Donnelley, and I went to the Outer Banks, we went with Brandon's family. Outer Banks is off the coast of North Carolina, it is a strip of land meant to protect the mainland from storm surges. It is probably one of the most beautiful places I have been to. There are beaches everywhere, everyone is so nice and the weather was amazing during the week I was there.

I was in Outer Banks for a full week, we stayed in a beach house and it was so perfect. Our house had a pool and a hot tub but it was great because it was in walking distance of common where there was a resturaunt, basketball courts, beach volleyball, and it was where everyone hung out. But the best part was that the beach was in our backyard, all we had to do was walk out our back door and the beach was right there. It was definitely a summer to remember.
